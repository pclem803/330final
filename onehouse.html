OneHouse

OneHouse was an application built by me and my teamates in an Agile Development Class my Sophomore Year at Northwestern. The class concentrated on learning and implementing agile methods into a React web app. Being all college students, our team wanted to create an a web app that helped other students coordinate shopping with their roomates. Being a part of this team was a fantastic experience, as it was one of the first times I felt like I was experiencing CS as practiced at the industry level.
